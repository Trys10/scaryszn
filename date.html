Medina Haunted Farm


Location:
Cumberland Street Medina, TN 38355



Details:
Located off the 45 Bypass on Cumberland Street in Medina, the farm features a wide range of ghostly ghouls, witches, cobwebs,

chainsaws, coffins and things that go bump in the country night, all while supporting a worthwhile cause. Tempt the evil spirits as you

walk a darkened trail, venturing in and out of nightmarish scenes of pure unspeakables. Venture into the House of Horror, if you dare, to

encounter nocturnal creeps and fear mongers. Parking and high-pitched screams are all free, and concessions will be available to be purchased.



What the money goes toward:
The Medina Lions Club puts the Haunted Farm each year to help raise funds that stay in the local area!
